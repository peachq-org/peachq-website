# Developers, we need you

PeachQ is about more than building a runtime. We're building an open ecosystem.

<!-- more -->

![Developers, we need you — help build the open q ecosystem](/img/peachq-we-need-you.png){ width="420" }

I'm looking for people interested in:

- **IDEs and editor integrations** — see
  [Confirmation PeachQ works](https://github.com/peachq-org/peachq/issues/12),
  which tracks what runs where
- **Libraries and tooling** — the
  [q-stdlib discussion](https://github.com/peachq-org/q-stdlib/discussions/2) is
  working out the principles and scope of a shared standard library
- **Documentation and tutorials** —
  [contributors welcome](https://github.com/peachq-org/peachq-website/issues/2)
  for tutorials, docs and news articles
- **Website and design**
- **Compatibility testing** — confirming what works on your platform is one of
  the most useful things you can do right now
- **Packaging and CI**

If you've built anything for q, or would like to, I'd love to hear from you.
